We used different forms and aspects to show datas as they are. The main idea was to illustrate the concept of being in its own world rather than thinking “out of the box”.

USA

For videos of less than 30K views, a lot of categories are mixt up and we can’t really see a main color. However, the more videos have views, the more we can see a majority of entertainment.

France

There is clearly a dominant color whatever the number of views is, the pink pale one which represents comedy. An interesting data regarding to other countries that almost haven’t any comedy color but much more of music or film.

Russia

Two trends emerge : videos of less than 30K views include categories as sport, music and activism whereas videos of more than 70K views are mostly for news.

Japan

Even though there are a lot of pink color which represents music category, we can find several time the same color like blue or orange for education and gaming.
